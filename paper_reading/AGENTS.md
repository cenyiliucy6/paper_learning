# Project Instructions

This project is used for daily literature monitoring and research planning in plant single-cell biology.

When the user asks to collect, summarize, compare, or interpret recent progress in any of the following topics:

- potato single-cell RNA-seq
- potato single-nucleus RNA-seq
- Solanum tuberosum single-cell omics
- potato stolon or tuber development
- plant anther single-cell sequencing
- pollen development
- plant reproductive tissue single-cell atlas
- plant single-cell ATAC-seq
- plant Multiome
- plant spatial transcriptomics
- plant single-cell data analysis

follow the skill instructions in:

./.codex/skills/plant-single-cell-daily-digest/SKILL.md

Default behavior:

- Use Chinese.
- Provide citations or source links when possible.
- Do not fabricate papers, DOIs, authors, journals, datasets, sample sizes, or conclusions.
- Clearly distinguish original research, review, method paper, dataset paper, and preprint.
- Always include a section called “领域进展动态总结”.
- The “领域进展动态总结” section should be updated every day and should summarize the field-level trend, not just list papers.
- Connect each relevant paper to the user's future PhD topic design.
- Prioritize potato, Solanaceae, anther, pollen, plant reproductive tissues, plant snRNA-seq, Multiome, and spatial transcriptomics.
- If results are too few in the last 7 days, expand to the last 30 days.
- If still too few, include representative papers from the last 6–12 months.
